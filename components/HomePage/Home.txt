Homepage Layout for Travel Website
----------------------------------

1. Hero Section: Capture attention and enable quick search
   - Full-width hero banner (background image or video)
   - Tagline: “Explore the World, One Journey at a Time”
   - Optional search bar:
        • “Where to?”
        • “Select Type (Tours / Activities / Cabs)”
        • Search button
   - CTA buttons: “Explore India”, “View International Packages”

2. Quick Access / Highlights
   - Quick navigation to main service areas:
        • Domestic Tours
        • International Tours
        • Day Activities
        • Cab Rentals

3. Popular Destinations in India
   - Show top Indian states:
        • Kerala
        • Goa
        • Himachal Pradesh
        • Rajasthan
   - Include “View All” link to /india

4. International Highlights
   - Show top international destinations:
        • Bali
        • Dubai
        • Switzerland
        • France
   - Include “View All” link to /abroad

5. Featured Packages / Tours
   - Section title: “Handpicked Tours for You”
   - Display carousel or grid of tours
   - Each tour card:
        • Image
        • Title
        • Short description
        • Starting price
        • “View Details” link

6. Explore by Theme
   - Section title: “Explore by Theme”
   - Example themes:
        • Honeymoon
        • Adventure
        • Family
        • Wildlife
        • Pilgrimage

7. Testimonials / Reviews
   - Highlight customer experiences and ratings

8. Blog Highlights
   - Section titled “Travel Stories & Guides”
   - Show 3–4 featured blogs with images and snippets
   - “Read More” or “Visit Blog” button

9. CTA Banner
   - Section encouraging bookings or newsletter signup
   - Example: “Ready for Your Next Adventure? Let’s Go!”

10. Footer
    - Company info, contact, social links
    - Quick links to About, Terms, Privacy Policy, FAQ, etc.
